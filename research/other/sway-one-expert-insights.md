## Key Expert Insights:

### 1. YouTube is Still Early for B2B
YouTube is still in early stages for B2B marketing — estimated 3-4 more years before most B2B companies take the platform seriously. This is an opportunity for early movers.

### 2. Strategy Over Presence
Many companies have a YouTube channel but few have a clear strategy. Having a channel ≠ having a strategy.

### 3. Play the Long Game
YouTube works but only if you play the long game — like SEO, not instant success. Videos compound and generate results over time.

### 4. Identify Patterns
Identify patterns in most successful videos to refine content strategy. Data-driven iteration > guessing.

### 5. SEO is the Entry Point
Most companies start YouTube as an SEO play — this is a valid and effective entry strategy.

### 6. Community Building is Advanced
Building community through YouTube is an advanced approach. Start with SEO, graduate to community.

### 7. Content Types That Work for B2B
Talking heads and repurposing content are effective B2B YouTube content types.

### 8. The Podcast-to-YouTube Pipeline
B2B podcasts are going video-first. Executive-hosted podcasts drive 6x more views.
