## Core Framework: 5 Stages of Customer Awareness
*(From Eugene Schwartz's "Breakthrough Advertising")*

### Stage 1: Unaware
People don't realize they have a problem. Use broad general keywords to draw attention.

### Stage 2: Problem-Aware
Know the problem exists but not the solutions. Content should validate the pain.

### Stage 3: Solution-Aware
Know solutions exist but haven't found your product. Comparison content, "how to" guides.

### Stage 4: Product-Aware
Know your product but haven't decided yet. Case studies, demos, social proof.

### Stage 5: Most-Aware
Ready to buy, need final push. Pricing pages, free trial CTAs, onboarding content.

---

## Key Takeaways:
- Rob wasted thousands of dollars taking wrong approach marketing Drip in early days
- Each awareness stage needs different content types AND promotion channels
- Framework applies directly to B2B SaaS content marketing
- Success KPIs differ for each stage
- Stage-specific content targeting ideal customer is essential
